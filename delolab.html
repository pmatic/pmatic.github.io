Delo Lab

BIO26|Common Knowledge, Ljubljana Biennial of Design, Museum of Architecture and Design, 2019

Your stories make our common history

Delo Lab is an open collaborative research platform rebuilding collective memory to create new narratives, rediscover common history and reconnect the community. In the time of an information crisis, dominated by short attention spans and fake news, both communities and archives remain sources of knowledge we can still rely on.

In Delo Lab, the newspaper archive is opened to the community and uses audience feedback to improve its collections with previously unpublished stories and information. Audience members have the opportunity to tell their personal histories and exchange their perspectives, which are then placed in the greater narrative of the community.

The Delo Lab platform offers space to diverse voices, acting as a platform for dialogue between various groups and individuals, thus generating new, crowdsourced knowledge.
