About me

My background is in museum and heritage studies, phonetics, European languages, and travel management.

I have collaborated with art and education organizations, public media, and city councils, as well as with frontline activists and community solidarity projects. After having worked in a French refugee camp, I decided to switch to cultural activism and commit to building platforms of change, connection, and dialogue that can involve a wider audience.
