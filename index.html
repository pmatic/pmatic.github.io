Petra Matić (*1988) is a museologist and activist.

She studied under ruangrupa, Bureau d'Études, and at the University of Zagreb.

She works with the histories of the Non-Aligned Movement in Zagreb, talks to museums about welcoming displaced people, and runs Jutro, a non-profit dedicated to arts, heritage, and human rights.

Her research is supported by the Gwärtler Foundation.
